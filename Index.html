Best College Student Recipes

Discover easy and delicious recipes that every college student can make. From quick breakfasts to hearty dinners, find your next favorite meal!

Breakfast

Overnight Oats

A quick and healthy breakfast option.

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Fresh fruits for topping

Mix all ingredients in a jar, refrigerate overnight, and enjoy in the morning!

Banana Pancakes

Delicious and fluffy pancakes perfect for a weekend brunch.

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Mash the banana and mix all ingredients together. Cook on a hot griddle until golden brown.

Lunch

Avocado Toast

A simple yet satisfying lunch option.

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, feta cheese

Toast the bread, mash the avocado, and spread it on the toast. Add your favorite toppings and enjoy!

Chicken Salad

A healthy and refreshing meal.

  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup celery, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grapes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Mix all ingredients in a bowl and serve on lettuce leaves or bread.

Dinner

Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish.

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cook the spaghetti. In a separate pan, cook the bacon and garlic. Mix the eggs and cheese together. Combine everything and serve immediately.

Stir-fry Vegetables

A quick and easy dinner packed with veggies.

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Optional: tofu or chicken

Heat the oil in a pan, add the garlic, and stir-fry the vegetables until tender. Add soy sauce and optional protein, cook for another 2 minutes, and serve.

Snacks

Granola Bars

Perfect for a quick snack on the go.

  • 2 cups oats
  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup nuts

Mix all ingredients, press into a pan, refrigerate for 1 hour, and cut into bars.

Fruit Smoothie

A refreshing and healthy drink.

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey

Blend all ingredients until smooth and enjoy!
